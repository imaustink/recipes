# Bakery Style Blueberry Muffins

## Ingredients

### Crumble Topping Ingredients
1. 4 tbsp butter, melted
2. 1/2 cup sugar
3. 1/2 cup flour
4. Sprinkle of cinnamon
5. Sprinkle of salt

### Muffin Ingredients
1. 1 stick (1/2 cup) of softened butter
2. 1 cup white sugar
3. 2 eggs
4. 1 tbsp lemon juice
5. 2 tsp vanilla extract
6. 2 cups flour
7. Sprinkle of salt
8. 2 tsp baking powder
9. 1/2 cup milk
10. 2 cups blueberries

## Directions

### Crumble Topping Instructions
1. Stir all ingredients with a fork until clumped together. Set aside.

### Muffin Instructions
1. In a bowl, combine butter and sugar and mix until creamed together.
2. Add eggs, lemon juice, and vanilla extract.
3. Sift flour into a separate bowl with a sprinkle of salt and baking powder, slowly mix into butter/egg/sugar mixture.
4. Add milk and make sure not to over mix.
5. Coat 2 cups blueberries lightly with flour in a separate bowl.
6. Mix coated blueberries into batter with hands or very gently with a spoon.
7. Scoop batter mix into liners, add crumble on top of batter.
8. Bake at 375Â°F for 30 minutes.

## Equipment

1. Mixing bowls
2. Fork
3. Sifter
4. Spoon
5. Muffin liners
6. Oven

## Tips

1. Ensure butter is softened for easier mixing.
2. Do not over mix the batter to keep muffins light and fluffy.
3. Coating blueberries with flour helps prevent them from sinking to the bottom of the muffins.
