# Whipped Feta Crostini with Hot Honey

## Ingredients

#### For the Whipped Feta:
1. 4 oz feta cheese, crumbled
2. 2 oz cream cheese, softened
3. 1 tablespoon olive oil
4. 1 teaspoon lemon juice
5. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

#### For the Crostini:
1. 1 baguette, sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
2. 2 tablespoons olive oil

#### For Topping the Crostinis:
1. Hot honey for drizzling
2. Chopped pistachios

## Directions

1. **Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).** Arrange baguette slices on a baking sheet and brush both sides lightly with olive oil. Bake for 8–10 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden and crisp. Let cool slightly.
2. **In a food processor,** blend feta, cream cheese, olive oil, lemon juice, and black pepper until smooth and creamy. Scrape down sides as needed. Chill until ready to use.
3. **To assemble,** spread a spoonful of whipped feta onto each crostini. Drizzle with hot honey and sprinkle with chopped pistachios. Serve immediately.

## Equipment

1. Oven
2. Baking sheet
3. Food processor
4. Brush for olive oil

## Tips

- Add toppings like fresh herbs or roasted grapes for extra flair.
- Whipped feta can be made up to 2 days in advance and stored in the fridge.
- Adjust the amount of hot honey to suit your taste preference.
