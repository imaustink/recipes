# Sticky Gochujang Banh Mi Sauce

## Ingredients

1. 2 cups low-sodium soy sauce
2. 1/2 cup brown sugar
3. 1 tsp fish sauce
4. 3 tbsp mirin
5. 2 tbsp rice vinegar
6. 2 tbsp black vinegar
7. 4 tbsp Shaoxing wine
8. 1 tsp MSG
9. 1/2 tsp white pepper
10. 1 tsp black peppercorns
11. 1 tbsp of gochujang
12. One piece of ginger, peeled and roughly chopped
13. 2 shallots, roughly chopped
14. 8 cloves of garlic, roughly chopped
15. 5 scallions, roughly chopped

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients in a pot.
2. Simmer on low heat until reduced to about half, approximately 3 hours.
   - The sauce should reach a thick, syrupy consistency.

## Equipment

1. Pot
2. Stove
3. Knife
4. Cutting board

## Tips

Ensure to stir occasionally to prevent sticking and burning. Adjust the heat as necessary to maintain a gentle simmer.
