# Homemade Pumpkin Sauce for Pumpkin Spice Lattes

## Ingredients

1. 1 cup brown sugar
2. 1/2 cup real pumpkin
3. 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
4. 1/8 teaspoon cloves
5. 1 heaping teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
6. 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
7. 2 tablespoons caramel syrup
8. 1/2 cup water
9. Pinch of salt
10. 2 tablespoons white chocolate sauce

## Directions

1. Start by adding 1 cup of brown sugar to a pot.
2. Add 1/2 cup of real pumpkin to the pot.
3. Add 1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon.
4. Add 1/8 teaspoon of cloves.
5. Add 1 heaping teaspoon of pumpkin pie spice.
6. Add 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract.
7. Add 2 tablespoons of caramel syrup.
8. Add 1/2 cup of water.
9. Add a pinch of salt.
10. Set the pot to medium-high heat and whisk until the mixture reaches a boil.
11. Let it boil for about 30 seconds while stirring.
12. Reduce the heat to low and add 2 tablespoons of white chocolate sauce.
13. Simmer on low for about 2 minutes.
14. Turn off the heat and let the sauce cool for 15 minutes.

## Equipment

1. Pot
2. Whisk
3. Measuring cups and spoons

## Tips

Use about an ounce of this pumpkin sauce when making a pumpkin spice latte. Store the sauce in the fridge for up to a week. Make sure to use real pumpkin for the best flavor.
