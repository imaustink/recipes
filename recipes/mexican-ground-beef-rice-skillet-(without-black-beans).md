# Mexican Ground Beef Rice Skillet (Without Black Beans)

## Ingredients

1. 1 tbsp olive oil
2. 1 small yellow onion, diced
3. 1 bell pepper, deseeded and diced
4. 1 lb lean ground beef
5. 2 cloves garlic, minced
6. 1 packet taco seasoning of choice
7. 1 cup rice
8. 3 1/2 cups chicken or beef broth
9. 1 cup corn
10. 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
11. Favorite garnishes, such as avocado and cilantro

## Directions

1. Heat olive oil in a large pan with sides over medium heat. Add the onion and bell pepper and saut√© for 5 minutes.
2. Add the ground beef, break it up with a wooden spoon and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat, if necessary.
3. Add garlic and taco seasoning and stir to combine. Add rice and broth, stir and bring everything to a gentle boil.
4. Cover and cook for 12 minutes, stirring every few minutes to prevent sticking.
5. Add corn and cook another 2-3 minutes, or until the rice is tender.
6. Turn the heat to low, top with cheese, cover with lid and cook until the cheese has melted, about 3-4 minutes.
7. Top with desired garnishes such as avocado and cilantro.

## Equipment

1. Large pan with sides
2. Wooden spoon
3. Lid for the pan

## Tips

This dish is perfect for meal prep and can be stored for leftovers. Customize with your favorite garnishes for added flavor.
